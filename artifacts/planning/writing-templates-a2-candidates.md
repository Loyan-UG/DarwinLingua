# Writing Templates A2 Candidate Plan

Status: planned after A1 completion, before A2 batch generation.

Quality gates:
- German source remains canonical.
- Titles do not repeat CEFR or content-family metadata.
- Helper translations are semantic and learner-facing, not word-by-word.
- A2 texts may use short reasons, polite requests, and simple connectors such as `weil`, `aber`, and `deshalb`.
- Linked practice uses only existing slugs.
- Each batch stays small enough for manual translation review.
