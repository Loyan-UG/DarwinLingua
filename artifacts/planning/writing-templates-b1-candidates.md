# Writing Templates B1 Candidate Plan

Status: planned after A1/A2 completion, before B1 batch generation.

Quality gates:
- German source remains canonical.
- Titles do not repeat CEFR or content-family metadata.
- Helper translations are semantic and learner-facing, not word-by-word.
- B1 templates should support longer but still reusable texts: clear paragraphs, reason, consequence, request, evidence, example, and appropriate closing.
- B1 writing must stay practical and exam-relevant without copying official exam prompts, model answers, or copyrighted tasks.
- Linked practice uses only existing slugs.
- Each batch stays small enough for manual translation review.
