# Exam Prep C2 Title Candidates

## Purpose

Reviewed C2 Exam Prep candidate backlog. C2 content must train expert-level precision, synthesis, style control, ambiguity handling, and spontaneous but disciplined argumentation without copying official provider tasks.

## Generation Rules

- Source titles, descriptions, explanations, strategy notes, and checklists are German-first.
- Helper translations cover `en`, `fa`, `ar`, `tr`, `ru`, `ckb`, `kmr`, `pl`, `ro`, and `sq`.
- Do not copy official provider prompts, transcripts, answer keys, model answers, or sample exam material.
- Do not put CEFR level, provider, or section into the learner-facing `title`.
- Link only imported and reviewed slugs. Writing Template links stay empty until real templates exist.
- C2 helper translations must be semantic and reader-friendly; avoid literal transfer of German abstractions such as `Souveraenitaet`, `Einordnung`, `Dichte`, `Nuance`, or `Register`.
