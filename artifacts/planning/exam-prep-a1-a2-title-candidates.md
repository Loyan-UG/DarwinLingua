# Exam Prep A1/A2 Title Candidates

## Purpose

Reviewed A1/A2 Exam Prep candidate backlog for the first regenerated foundation packages. Titles must read like clean learner tasks; CEFR level, provider, section, and task type stay in metadata.

## Generation Rules

- Source titles, descriptions, explanations, strategy notes, and checklists are German-first.
- Helper translations cover `en`, `fa`, `ar`, `tr`, `ru`, `ckb`, `kmr`, `pl`, `ro`, and `sq`.
- Do not copy official provider tasks, prompts, transcripts, answer keys, or sample exam material.
- Link only imported and reviewed slugs. Writing Template links stay empty until real Writing Template content exists.
- Use `linkedRoleplaySlugs` for standalone RoleplayScenario practice; do not put roleplay slugs into dialogue links.
