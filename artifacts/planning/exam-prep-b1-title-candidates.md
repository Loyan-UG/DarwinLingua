# Exam Prep B1 Title Candidates

## Purpose

Reviewed B1 Exam Prep candidate backlog. Titles are learner-facing tasks; provider, CEFR level, section, and task type stay in metadata and filters.

## Generation Rules

- Source titles, descriptions, explanations, strategy notes, and checklists are German-first.
- Helper translations cover `en`, `fa`, `ar`, `tr`, `ru`, `ckb`, `kmr`, `pl`, `ro`, and `sq`.
- Do not copy official provider tasks, prompts, transcripts, answer keys, or sample exam material.
- Link only imported and reviewed slugs. B1 Writing Template links stay empty until real templates exist.
- Prefer B1 strategies: complete task coverage, clear structure, polite repair language, everyday/workplace register, and simple but meaningful connectors.
