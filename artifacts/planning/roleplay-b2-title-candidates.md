# B2 RoleplayScenario Title Candidates

Date: 2026-05-26

## Purpose

This file preserves the reviewed B2 standalone `RoleplayScenario` title backlog before full multilingual content generation. Use it to avoid duplicate titles, B1-level simplicity, English-first metadata, weak answer-choice design, unsupported controlled values, and ordinary dialogue filler in future B2 roleplay batches.

## B2 Generation Rules

- Source `title`, `description`, and `learnerGoal` must be German-first.
- Add learner-language translations later for `en`, `fa`, `ar`, `tr`, `ru`, `ckb`, `kmr`, `pl`, `ro`, and `sq`.
- Keep B2 scenarios deterministic and scripted: no AI chat, no free-text scoring, and no learner-generated text sent to a model.
- B2 scenarios should usually have 10-12 turns and at least 4 answer-choice moments.
- B2 learner turns should require nuanced explanation, disagreement, negotiation, indirect criticism, prioritization, summary, and polite repair.
- Recommended learner turns should appear in the scripted turn sequence.
- Answer choices must include at least one correct deterministic choice for validation, but learner-facing UI should emphasize plausible weaker/not-recommended alternatives instead of repeating the correct answer.
- Incorrect choices must be plausible but insufficient: too blunt, too vague, missing a reason, missing evidence, missing compromise, missing next step, register-inappropriate, or too simple for B2. Do not use irrelevant filler such as `Ich weiß nicht.`.
- B2 tasks should focus on workplace, public-office, housing, health, school, service, travel, social nuance, and exam discussion situations.
- Use only importer-supported controlled values for `taskType`, `skillFocus`, `interactionMode`, and `register`.
- Use active topic keys, for example `everyday-life`, `social-and-relationships`, `shopping-and-services`, `appointments-and-health`, `public-services`, `transport-and-travel`, `education-and-training`, `customer-service`, `work-and-jobs`, `housing`, `business-communication`, `documents-and-administration`, and `meetings-and-presentations`.
