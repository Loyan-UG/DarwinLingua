# Writing Templates A1 Candidate Plan

Status: planned after Exam Prep completion, before bulk generation.

Quality gates:
- German source remains canonical.
- Titles do not repeat CEFR or content-family metadata.
- Helper translations are semantic, not literal.
- Linked practice uses only existing slugs.
- Each pilot batch stays small enough for manual translation review.
