# Grammar Syllabus A1-C2 v1

Repository target path: `content/learning-portal/grammar/syllabus/grammar-syllabus-a1-c2-v1.json`

This syllabus is the source map for grammar content generation. Each topic has a stable `slug` / `contentKey` that Codex/importers should use for upsert behavior.

## Target Languages

- `en` — English: Use clear learner-friendly explanations and compare with English word order when useful.
- `fa` — Persian: Explain through Persian grammar intuition; point out where German differs from Persian, especially articles, cases, and word order.
- `ar` — Arabic: Use Arabic grammar comparisons carefully; explain German case/article behavior separately from Arabic definite article logic.
- `tr` — Turkish: Compare with Turkish agglutinative structure only when helpful; emphasize German articles, case endings, and word order differences.
- `ru` — Russian: Use Russian case-system intuition where useful, but explain German article/case interaction explicitly.
- `ckb` — Kurdish Sorani: Use simple Sorani explanations and avoid assuming strong grammatical terminology unless introduced.
- `kmr` — Kurdish Kurmanji: Use Kurmanji-friendly explanations and highlight German article/case differences.
- `pl` — Polish: Use Polish case-system intuition where useful; clarify differences in articles and German word order.
- `ro` — Romanian: Compare article/gender concepts carefully and show German-specific article placement and cases.
- `sq` — Albanian: Use Albanian-friendly explanations and explicitly introduce German article/case concepts.
